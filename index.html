  • My Yoga Practice

    I teach hatha, vinyasa flow, and yin yoga. I also offer yoganidra, an advanced meditative practice. My classes are infused with playfulness, creativity, and balance. My goal is to leave students feeling safe, invigorated, and self-expressed.
